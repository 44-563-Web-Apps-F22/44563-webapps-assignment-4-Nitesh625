Nitesh Bellamkonda

CHORUS

(appalled).

When two doughty heroes thunder, All the world is lost in wonder; When such men their temper lose, Awful are the words they use!



LUD.
Tall snobs, small snobs, rich snobs and needy ones!



RUD.

(jostling him).

Whom are you alluding to?



LUD.

(jostling him).

Where are you intruding to?



RUD.
Fat snobs, thin snobs, swell snobs and seedy ones!



LUD.
I rather think you err. To whom do you refer?
